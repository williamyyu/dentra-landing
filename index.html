The Solution

Dentra is a WhatsApp-based assistant for dental clinics. The moment a call is missed, it automatically reaches out to the patient — recovering the conversation before they book elsewhere. Your receptionist stays in control. Nothing about how you run the clinic needs to change.

1
Missed call detected
Your landline forwards missed calls to Dentra. Within 15 seconds, the patient receives a WhatsApp from your clinic's number.
2
Patient books via native WhatsApp form
Patient taps "Book appointment" — a native WhatsApp form opens. They fill in name, date, and preferred time in one go. No back-and-forth.
3
Receptionist confirms
Booking requests arrive as a structured WhatsApp notification. One tap confirms — patient receives instant confirmation.
