The Problem
Dental clinics are quietly losing tens of thousands of dollars every year — without realising it.
When a patient calls and nobody answers, they don't wait. They open Google and book with the next clinic. When a booked patient doesn't show up, that chair sits empty and unbillable. Both problems are preventable. Neither gets enough attention — because they're invisible until you do the numbers.

The Solution

Dentra is a missed-call recovery workflow for dental clinics. The moment a call is missed, it reaches out on WhatsApp and captures what the patient wants before they disappear. Your receptionist stays in control. Dentra handles the first response and hands off the request cleanly.

1
Missed call detected
Your landline forwards missed calls to Dentra. Within 30 seconds, the patient receives a WhatsApp from your clinic's number.
2
Patient sends a booking request in WhatsApp
Patient taps "Book appointment" and completes a native WhatsApp form with name, preferred date, and preferred time window. No back-and-forth.
3
Reception follows up with the real slot
Reception receives a structured request, checks the real calendar, and confirms the actual appointment manually.
